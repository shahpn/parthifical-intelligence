X

PARAM is a project put together by several CMPSC 445 Students at Penn State Abington! We were tasked with utilizing Machine Learning in a creative way, and we had the fantastic idea of building an AI chatbot. We wanted to build a chatbot to help advise Penn State Students, and thus, PARAM was born! PARAM can help you learn more about classes you plan to take, help you find things on campus, keep up to date with your assignments, and even contact your professors!

Instructions: Simply type your message in the bar at the bottom of your screen, and then hit enter! PARAM will respond to the best of its ability!
Updates: May 2nd, 2023 - Beta Release: PARAM can now access the PSU Bulletin to help you find out more about any CMPSC or IST course you wish!
